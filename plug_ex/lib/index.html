Welcome page

Elixir is a dynamic, functional language designed for building scalable and maintainable applications. Elixir leverages the Erlang VM, known for running low-latency, distributed and fault-tolerant systems, while also being successfully used in web development and the embedded software domain. To learn more about Elixir, check our getting started guide and our learning page for other resources. Or keep reading to get an overview of the platform, language and tools.
